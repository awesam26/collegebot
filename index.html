Dr. G Viswanathan

Founder – Chancellor of VIT University

The VIT legacy, which started from Vellore Engineering College 35 years ago to four Universities in India, today, is a result of constant efforts to impart high quality of education. The Govt. of India has recognized VIT as No. 1 Private Institution for Innovation, ARIIA 2019, and the recent recognition, as an Institution of Eminence, has paved a way to focus more on research with international collaborations to move up in the global ranking and contribute in the capacity building of our nation.

Future Ready Programmes

Aerospace Engineering

An intriguing area of Mechanical Engineering in the realm of aircraft design and development.

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Designed to empower students to make systems adaptive through artificial intelligence and deep learning to meet the industry challenges.

Bio Engineering

An alluring interface between engineering and medicine designed to address real-world biological and medical problems using modern engineering techniques.

Cyber Security & Digital Forensics

A foray into the cyber-space with a focus on protection of digital assets and intelligence.

Gaming Technology

Tantalizing blend of software and technology delving through the rapidly evolving field of Game Design, Development and Virtual Reality.

Our New Programmes

AI & Cybernetics

Cybernetics – a science dealing with the systems of controlling processes in animate and inanimate objects, organisms, machines.

Cloud Computing & Automation

Cloud Automation refers to the process of leveraging technology tools and resources to reduce manual burden in provisioning and managing cloud computing workloads

Education Technology

E-Learning has become a booming industry that gives an avenue to learn anytime and anywhere. Several Instruction Design Companies generate revenue in terms of providing solutions through AI-enabled software

E-Commerce Technology

E-Commerce 2025 has started with the paradigm shift from online shopping to voice shopping, visual search(AR/VR), smart payments (IoT, AI), crypto payments, smart customer support(chatbots) etc.

Health Informatics

Health informatics is a multidisciplinary specialization dealing with the collection and management of health data. The techniques of Computational data analysis and AI are used for developing effective healthcare solutions. It is an emerging field in the post-corona world.

AI & Robotics

An interdisciplinary area where one can learn about AI an Emerging technology with the Robotics.

B.Arch

Vernacular to Virtual Reality based Adaptive Architecture incorporates both digital technology and new thinking in a sustainable manner to showcase the unique identity of every Architectural Design. With the Unique ability to combine architectural education with well advanced computer graphics, animations, Automations and 3 D graphics with printing and much more use of the latest technologies for Energy simulations, Environmental applications and Climate responsive designing features , VIT School of Architecture will be of its own kind to start the highly combated course of Architecture with modern applications all sort of computer aided designing.
